About Me

Hey! I'm M. Boopal, a passionate Electronics & Communication Engineering student at Vels Institute of Science, Technology & Advanced Studies (VISTAS), Chennai.

I started this blog to document what I learn — because the best way to understand something is to explain it. You'll find notes on labs, project breakdowns, study resources, and my honest take on ECE concepts.

When I'm not debugging circuits, I'm probably reading about 5G networks, embedded Linux, or RF design.
